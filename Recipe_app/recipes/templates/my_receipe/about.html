About Us

Welcome to "Hamro Recipe Haru", a recipes sharing platform! Our project allows users to explore and share delicious recipes with each other. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a cooking enthusiast, our platform provides a space for you to discover new recipes and connect with other users who share your passion for cooking.

Key Features

  • Browse and search recipes from all users in the same local network
  • Create, update, and delete your own recipes
  • Secure user authentication system for registrations and logins

Technologies Used

This project is built using the following technologies:

  • Python/Django for backend development
  • HTML/CSS/ for frontend development
  • SQLite database for storing recipe data

Future Plans

We are continuously working on improving our platform and adding new features to enhance the user experience. In the future, we plan to introduce features such as:

  • Advanced search functionality to filter recipes by ingredients, cuisines, and dietary preferences
  • Integration with social media platforms for easier sharing and networking
  • Recipe recommendation system based on user preferences and activity
  • Interact with other users by commenting and rating recipes

Thank you for being a part of our recipe-sharing community!
